Centre D'Amitié, founded in 1987 by Soeur Bernadette Camoin in Bambous, focuses on early child development for ages 3 months to 6 years. Committed to eradicating poverty, it provides a nurturing environment, nutritious meals, and support, enabling parents to earn. The initiative thrives on sponsor generosity.
